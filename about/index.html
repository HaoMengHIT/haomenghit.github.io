Meng Hao

Meng Hao received the BS degree in computer science and engineering from Harbin Institute of Technology, China, in 2014. He is currently working toward the PhD degree in the School of Computer Science and Technology, Harbin Institute of Technology. His research interests include high performance computing, performance modeling, parallel optimization.

Email contact: haomeng (at) hit (dot) edu (dot) cn OR haomenghit (at) gmail (dot) com.
